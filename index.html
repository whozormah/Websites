Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my own menu or make changes to yours

Yes, we can work together to curate a customised menu based on your taste.

Can you provide for vegetarian and special dietary needs?

Yes, we do; especially without stock cubes, using sea salt & natural seasoning.

How far in advance should I reserve my date?

We have had bookings a year ahead in our experience. To guarantee, and lock in, we will require you to deposit a percentage of the agreed total value of the job. Terms & Conditions apply.

Can you provide rental items for my event?

For some catering equipment, yes we can.

Do you provide water, juice, soft drinks, wine or liquor?

For small capacity engagements, we can provide, but for large ones, Dunnice Events, our events affiliate that handles full event planning, management, coordination and vendor sourcing, will recommend a vendor whose specialty is drinks management.

Do your servers wear uniforms?

Yes, they do. We have different outfits to suit the occasion and your preference.

Do you set up and clean up?

We set up guests’ tables, clean up the food serving points & wash area immediately after the event.

Do you go outside Lagos?

Yes, we do. We have been to all the five south west states of Nigeria numerously. In addition, we have served in Akwa Ibom, Edo, Enugu, Imo and Kwara States several times. We have also plied our trade in Ghana. We look forward to going to any part of the world to serve our dunnicious delicacies. We are counting on you to take us there!
